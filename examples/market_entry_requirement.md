# Southeast Asia Market Entry Requirement

Client is a US fintech startup exploring expansion into Singapore, Indonesia, and Vietnam.

Business context:
- Strong US product-market fit in SMB payments.
- No international operating model.
- Leadership wants an initial market entry recommendation within 6 weeks.

Known needs:
- Compare regulatory barriers by country.
- Identify payment partner options.
- Estimate go-to-market budget and timeline.
- Recommend whether to launch directly, partner, or defer.

Constraints:
- Legal team capacity is limited.
- Budget for discovery is capped at 75k USD.
- Board wants a decision memo before the next quarterly meeting.

Open questions:
- Which customer segment is highest priority in Asia?
- Is product localization required before launch?
- What risk tolerance does leadership have for regulatory uncertainty?
